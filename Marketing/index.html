Fall, Number… Fall!

A colorful falling-numbers puzzle game where tiles become sums. Match horizontal or vertical numbers, clear the board, and chase your best score.

Addition puzzle Beginner & Expert modes Colorful learning play

About the game

Guide falling number tiles onto the board and create sums that match the current target. When the numbers add up correctly, they disappear.

Features

  • Horizontal and vertical sum matching
  • Beginner mode for learning
  • Expert mode for a faster challenge
  • Power-ups: row clear, column clear, reorder
  • Cascades, perfect clears, and best scores

How to play

Move the falling tile left or right with horizontal swipes. Swipe down or double-tap to drop faster. Make the numbers add up to the target.

Horizontal example
4
3
5
4 + 3 + 5 = 12
Vertical example
7
2
3
7 + 2 + 3 = 12
